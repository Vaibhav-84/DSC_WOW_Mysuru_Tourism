Chamarajendra Zoo -

Sri Chamarajendra Zoological Gardens, popularly known as 'Mysore Zoo', is one of the oldest zoos of the country established in 1892, by his highness, the erstwhile ruler of Mysore Sri Chamarajendra Wadiyar Bahadur.

2017 -

Today Mysuru Zoo has 125 years of history and completely Moderninised without shifting the zoo from the original place. Housing more than 1450 specimens belonging to 168 species, representing more than 30 countries.
